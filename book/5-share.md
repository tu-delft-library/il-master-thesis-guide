# 5. Share

When handing in your thesis you have to take care that you are accurately using and reusing the work of others. If you are interested in a future career in research, look into additional ways to share your work by applying open science principles. 

![citing sources](5-write.png)

Common activities during this phase of the your information journey include:

- [5a. Citing Sources](5a-citing.md) - Understand ways of citing, apply a correct referencing style and create a reference list for a variety of sources
- [5b. Reporting AI Use](5b-specifying-ai-use.md) - Know why and how you should acknowledge the use of AI in your work
- [5c. Re(use) Copyrighted Sources](5c-images-code.md) - Correctly (re)use sources in accordance with their copyright licensing and know your own rights
- [5d. Open Science](5d-open-science.md) - Know about open science principles
